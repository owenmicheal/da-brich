DA’BRICH INTERNATIONAL LIMITED based in THE REPUBLIC OF UGANDA. This is an organization associated with helping the needy without profit-making motive and identifies a weaker section of the society and serve them by participation in their struggle for development.
Our Mission is to make the world a better place for everybody by empowering all women, families and community in vulnerable situations with the necessary support to promote their full well-being, physical and emotional development by providing them with free education, food, water, clothes and other basic necessities and also facilitate for public health and hospital care, family planning, maternity and child welfare nutrition, preventive and curative measures for endemic and epidemic diseases, health and family welfare education with the help of qualified personnel and without profit-making motive.
Our vision is to create positive and measurable change for women, families and community economic self-reliance through the implementation of sustainable programs and initiatives and to arrange educational programs to make the people critically conscious of their surroundings and to help them overcome the limitations and constraints under which they live.
